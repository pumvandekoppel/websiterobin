"Featuring the collaborative works of: Bo Deurloo & Mice and Bird, Robin Whitehouse & a spring market, Thordur Hans Baldursson & a queue of musicians, Emma Sebregts & Jeanne van Steenbergen, Pum van de Koppel & The ministry of finance, Roselyn Flach & Yoda, Suhjoo Kim & Jiyoung Yim, Matei Dragomir & Alper Çekinmez, Marion Haguet & Caty, Nanou, Delphine, Manu and Cécille

Over the last semester, as part of a long standing collaboration between the Fine Arts department and Nest, a number of our students from the 2nd, 3rd and 4th year have reacted to the exhibition ‘The Grand Palace of Everyone’, which closed last week. Borrowing similar motivations, ‘Peas and Carrots’ focusses on collaboration, with a person, being or force outside of themselves, opening up their practice to others.

The students were selected via open call as part of the Fine Art department electives and were guided in their process by three professional coaches: Jan Hoek, Clementine Edwards and Tim Hollander."
