Title

'gips book'

Year

2023

This publication records the act of returning 4 plaster sculptures to the Royal Academy of Art The Hague. Apollo Sauroktonos, 96,6 x 73,7 x 167,2 cm (16204) Diadoumenos door Polykleitos 102 x 59,5 x 191 cm (16151) Discuswerper van Naukydes 84 x 48,8 x 178 (16074)

On the day that this took place I documented the process using 3 handcams, the book includes screenshots from this somewhat happening.

Allard Pierson interview and show René van Beek (curator Allard Pierson museum) and I met in the basement of the academy 6 months after I loaned the sculptures from their collection. We had a discussion about the rise and fall of a collection and the responsibilities of an institution to tell the story. The film was on display in the “Verhalenkabinet” at the Allard Pierson from April-July2024. Shown next to a display of plaster fragments and a text panel written by René.
