Robin Phoenix Whitehouse (2000, UK)
's practice stems from an interest in working with relations and situations. His works addresses his presence in the current time and place, it looks back at what has happened as much as looking forward as to what is it to come. Working predominantly with traditional processes, but considering notions of them and somewhat questioning them in today’s scene. He’s currently living and working in Den Haag and spent the most past of this year in studying in Paris studying at the Beaux Arts, specialising in screen printing.
